PROJECTS

Video Games
Mood for a Melody
A fresh spin on the interactive story game genre, based on the classic Billy Joel song.
Voyage of the Infinity
The browser-based text adventure game and engine of my young game developer's dreams.
Cannoneer
An award-winning horizontal shooter with vertical shooter mechanics. Also, you play as a pirate ship.
Lost and Raturn
A fun little arcade game about a friendly rat who just wishes people would stop dropping all their stuff.
RFID Pong
Your favorite retro video game with a rather uncommon controller scheme.
Terminal
An award-winning asynchronous couch co-op game that combines the joy of platforming with the typing of a hacker.
Conflicting Objectives
A prototype for a multiplayer arcade game where you collect coins while trying to figure out where you're going.
EECSurvival & The DFQE
A simple top down shooter created on a homemade engine - the DFQE.
Minesweeper
A basic recreation of the classic Minesweeper game with an ambitious goal.
Webpages
Applican
A startup that aimed to connect employers with computer science students looking for entry-level jobs or internships.
WhatShouldICallFreddy.com
I figured I'd rename my dog of 14 years. Want to help?
Spotify Data Fix
Associated with the Spotify Data Analysis project below, this page was used to easily fix holes in downloaded Spotify data.
PoserRank
A revolutionary new social media platform where friends can rank each others' poser score!
Hot Rod DB
A database of gifs that I made for my roommate as a birthday present, all from the fantastic movie Hot Rod.
Doodle Clone
A web-based clone of the popular schedule-planning app, Doodle.
